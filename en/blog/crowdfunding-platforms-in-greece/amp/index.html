Crowdfunding Greece

The concept of crowdfunding is very much popular in Greece, and people are well versed in spending time on online fundraising sites for people who care about helping others. Through crowdfunding platforms, the population of Greece has been able to help raise a lot of funds for various organisations and individuals. Considering the Greek economy and many potential investors, thousands of people donate to various charitable, personal, and medical causes annually through fundraising websites.

Now when it comes to crowdfunding platforms Greece, there are many popular platforms ready to welcome you and give you the opportunity to start your campaign. However, we have managed to find the top 10 notable crowdfunding platforms in Greece that are reliable for beginning a campaign.

Experience the most smooth crowdfunding with WhyDonate. Start your crowdfunding campaign today!

Top 10 Crowdfunding Platforms In Greece

1. WhyDonate

 

One of the most popular platforms for fundraising in Greece is WhyDonate, which is used by individuals and organisations in over 20 European countries. It is free to sign up and allows people to raise money for various charitable activities. Besides being a convenient platform for individuals and organisations, WhyDonate also allows corporate fundraising and charitable activities.

With WhyDonate, people can easily create and manage their own fundraising campaigns. It also allows them to connect with other individuals and organisations. Its easy-to-use platform and its multiple active fundraisers make it an ideal choice for individuals and organisations.

WhyDonate also allows users to add their own custom branding and content to fundraisers, donation forms and emails. And guess what? Users can do all of this by downloading the mobile app for WhyDonate to track their activities efficiently. The fundraiser app of WhyDonate allows users to have all kinds of seamless experiences while crowdfunding for their causes while they are on the go.

Fees – 

  • Transaction Cost – 1.9%+ €0.25
  • WhyDonate charges 0% platform costs and no additional subscription costs.

2. Indiegogo

The crowdfunding platform, Indiegogo, has a good success rate and is a good choice for individuals who want to raise funds in Greece. It is available in over 200 countries and territories, making it an ideal choice for people who are looking to start a campaign.

With over 20 features and campaign options, the crowdfunding platform in Greece supports various types of organisations and individuals. With a fixed goal, users can expect to receive funds regardless of their raised amount.

Even if there is a 5% platform plus a 3% to 5% third-party processing fee, people still choose to use the platform due to the high success rate.

Fees –

  • Indiegogo has an international transfer fee of $25 when sending funds to banks outside of the United States.
  • Platform Fee – 5%
  • Processing Fee – 3% to 5%

3. JumpStart

JumpStart is one of the most popular crowdfunding platforms Greece. The goal of the platform is to provide a productive tool in Greece for creative and innovative people with ideas who lack the financial support to bring them to life. JumpStart is supported by the Greek Diaspora and Philhellenes, who believe that a sustainable economic recovery can only be achieved through job creation and innovation. Anyone can support creative artists who have great ideas through JumpStart by donating to their innovative ideas.

Fees – 

  • If a campaign is successfully funded – JumpStart fee – 8% of total funds raised
  • Payment processing fees – 3% -3.5% per pledge amount
  • No fees are charged in case of the campaign is not successful

4. GoGetFunding

GoGetFunding is a peer-to-peer platform that enables individuals to raise funds for various purposes. It was first launched in 2011 and has since become one of the most popular platforms for crowdfunding.

With GoGetFunding, users can raise funds for various purposes, and they can keep the money they get even if they don’t reach their goal. This is because it allows people to reach out to a wider circle of supporters.

Fees –

  • GoGetFunding Fee – 4%
  • Payment processing fee – 2.9%
  • Handling Fee per donation – 0.30c

5. Give Joy

Give Joy was founded in 2019 with the goal of enabling philanthropy by providing a platform for individuals and non-profit organisations to raise funds in order to achieve their goals. Through Give Joy, anyone can donate to any campaign without any complicated processes. Besides being a platform for philanthropy, Give Joy also allows individuals to create their own fundraising campaigns and reach their goals.

Fees –

  • 4% platform right + 3% PayPal costs

6. Act4Greece By NBG

Through its strategic partnership with various foundations and international organisations, the National Bank of Greece can support Act4Greece’s activities. Some of these include the John Latsis Foundation, Bodossaki Foundation, and Onassis Foundation.

The Act4Greece program aims to promote developmental and social banking. It supports projects that are focused on the welfare, health, and social solidarity of Greece’s citizens. It also aims to encourage entrepreneurship and culture, as well as provide training and education for young and innovative entrepreneurs.

One of Act4Greece’s main goals is to channel and broaden the scope of its activities, leveraging the resources of the international community. It supports projects that are focused on addressing the real needs of Greek society.

Fees –

  • Processing Fee (Other than NBG)

7.  Chuffed

Chuffed.org is a community-focused crowdfunding platform that enables individuals and groups to raise funds for socially-conscious projects. We support projects that are run by not-for-profit organisations, social enterprises, and community groups.

The company’s mission is to operate for the benefit of its community. As a social benefit organisation, the directors of Chuffed.org are expected to consider the impact of their decisions on the environment and society.

Fees –

  • The campaigner will pay the card fees (2-3%) plus a Chuffed.org platform fee (4.9% + GST/VAT) out of the donation.
  • Payment processing fees: 2.90% +€0.30

8. Kickstarter

Kickstarter is one of the most popular platforms for people to solve the problem of how to make money online in Greece when it comes to crowdfunding platforms. This is a Brooklyn-based platform where individuals can also start their own fundraising campaigns from all over the world, including Greece.

All of the pledges made through Kickstarter are considered to be all-or-nothing. Projects that reach their funding goal will not be charged a fee. This ensures that creators have a clear picture of their budget before they start working on their projects.

Fees –

  • A service fee of 5%, along with 3% + $0.20 per transaction for payment processing

9. Give&Fund

Founded by Ioannis Chatzibeis in 2013, Give&Fund is a crowdfunding platform to help people change their lives through its easy-to-use fundraising structure. It will also support projects that will help others. Give&Fund is one of the top crowdfunding platforms in Greece, where anyone can start a campaign or choose to do philanthropic activities by donating to active missions.

Fees –

  • G&F charge supply – 4.5 % + 0.20
  • PayPal 3.4 % +0,30 €
  • VAT charges

10. Fundable

Fundable is one of the leading business crowdfunding platforms in Greece that enables companies to raise capital. It was created by the founders of several successful startups who have raised millions of dollars from angel investors and venture capital. Fundable helps navigate the various challenges that come with running a business as the crowdfunding platform built exclusively for startups. 

Fundable offers a concierge package that includes a team of professional consultants and designers who will help build a compelling and effective investment strategy to align fundraising goals and create visual presentations in order to attract and retain investors.

Fees –

  • $179 per Month to Fundraise
  • Merchant processing charge – 3.5% + 30¢ per transaction.
